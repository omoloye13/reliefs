Surviving Winter

PAKISTAN FLOODS APPEAL

Extremely heavy rainfall in Pakistan has resulted in catastrophic flash flooding leaving one-third of the country underwater.

More than 33 million people have been affected

According to the most recent reports, over 1,600 people have tragically lost their lives, while approximately 220,000 homes have been destroyed.

Islamic Relief Pakistan is responding, providing vital humanitarian aid.

Please donate generously and help save lives.
